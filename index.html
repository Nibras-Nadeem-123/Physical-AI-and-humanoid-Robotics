This textbook is a comprehensive introduction to the exciting world of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and accessible resource for students, researchers, and enthusiasts.

We will cover a wide range of topics, from the fundamental principles of AI and robotics to the latest advancements in humanoid robot design and control. You will learn about the key technologies that are driving the field forward, including ROS 2, Gazebo, Unity, NVIDIA Isaac, and more.

We hope you enjoy this journey into the future of robotics!
